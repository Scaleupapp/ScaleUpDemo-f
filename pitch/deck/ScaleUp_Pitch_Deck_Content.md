## SLIDE 8 — THE AI THESIS

**AI commoditizes execution. Judgment is career-proof.**

*"68% of India's white-collar professionals expect AI to automate their jobs within 5 years"*
— Nithin Kamath, June 2025

**Tasks AI will do better in 2 years:** write code, draft reports, analyze data, create campaigns, solve exam problems

**What AI cannot do:** architect the right system, identify the right market, make decisions with incomplete information, evaluate trade-offs, lead teams

**ScaleUp doesn't train people to do tasks.**
**We train people to make decisions.**

Our C2O loop doesn't test *"can you do this?"*
It tests *"do you know WHY, WHEN, and WHETHER to do this?"*

**As AI gets stronger, ScaleUp becomes more essential — not less.**

*"When calculators replaced mental math, accountants didn't disappear — they became strategic advisors."*

**Speaker notes:** "This is the question every investor asks — in a world where AI does everything, why would anyone need ScaleUp? Here's the answer: AI commoditizes execution. But execution was never the hard part. Judgment was. [Read the two lists.] Our C2O loop is specifically designed to build and measure judgment — not task completion. The more powerful AI gets, the more the world needs people who can direct it. ScaleUp builds those people."
