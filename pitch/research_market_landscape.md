## WHY BYJU's / UNACADEMY / WHITEHAT JR FAILED — Investor "do not do" list

**Things investors now EXPLICITLY AVOID:**
1. Commission-based telesales engines ("call-center-as-edtech")
2. Content libraries with no measured outcomes (no RCTs, no learning-gain data)
3. Acquisition-driven growth without integration
4. Live tutoring at scale (broken unit economics)
5. Coding-for-kids / parent-anxiety models
6. Audit-delayed financials (Deloitte resigned at BYJU's after 18-month delay)
7. CAC > 12-month LTV
8. 40%+ marketing spend ratios

**What investors LOOK FOR ("post-BYJU edtech"):**
1. Demonstrated learning OUTCOMES (pre/post test data, not engagement)
2. Strong D30/D90 retention curves that flatten
3. CAC payback under 12 months, CM positive at unit level
4. Product-led growth (low paid-CAC dependence)
5. AI-NATIVE (not AI-bolted-on)
6. Vernacular + Tier 2/3 accessibility at right pricing
7. Founders with operator/teaching depth (not pure growth backgrounds)
8. PW thesis proven: sustainable growth + student trust → public markets

## AI-NATIVE LEARNING OUTCOMES EVIDENCE (peer-reviewed — gold for deck)

- **Khanmigo SY24-25:** 2.0M users (+731% YoY); partner district usage 68K → 700K
- **Duolingo Max (Q3 2025):** 9% of paid subs (~981K), Max drives ARPU growth
  - "Explain My Answer" → 34% better grammar retention
  - 65% adoption → +15% course completion
  - AI cohorts: 20-30% retention improvement
- **Coursera Coach:** 1M+ learners; +9.5% quiz pass rate; +11.6% lessons/hour
- **Wiley meta-analysis 2025:** GenAI has moderate-positive significant effect on learning outcomes
- **ChatGPT meta-analysis (51 studies):** LARGE positive effect on learning performance
- **Harvard Kestin RCT (June 2025):** AI tutor outperforms in-class active learning by 0.73-1.3 SD (anything >0.4 is "educationally significant")
- **NotebookLM:** ~28M visits over 3 months ending Feb 2025 — viral substitute for passive learning

**Counter-evidence to acknowledge:** Oct 2025 arXiv paper warns AI tools cause knowledge decrease + over-reliance when used PASSIVELY. **This validates ScaleUp's C2O loop thesis** — passive AI use is bad; active AI-coached outcomes are good.
